Introduction

Our project consists on making a Natural Compost.

This treatment consists on a solid mix of nutrients and active ingredients that people will distribute in their crops.

This mix will help people regenerate damaged soils, imporve the quality of crops and minimize the use of water by maximizing the performance of it.

Read More

Objectve

Our objective is to reduce hunger by helping people to produce their own crops using compost made of a solid mix of nutrients and active ingredients.

ONU Goal

The goal we are trying to help with is Goal 2: Zero Hunger, this goal is about trying to end any form of malnutrition and have inclusive and sustainable food sysytems.

Why These Materials?

We look for materials that have the properties needed for the regenerative effect we seek. We chose these materials, because we know they are full of nutrients that can then be positive for the plants.

Ingredients

All of our ingredients are 100% natural and full of benefits for the plants, this are all mixed together and then used to provide the minerals to the plant and assure much stronger roots, and a faster production rate.
